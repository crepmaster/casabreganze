Welcome to your weekly guide to things to do in Milan. Here is our pick to make the most of
the city during your stay at the EasyRest apartment.

## Exhibitions not to miss

The summer season brings a wave of exhibitions. Remember to book your tickets in advance,
especially on weekends.

## Concerts and nightlife

The Navigli come alive in the evening: bars, live music and aperitifs by the water.

## Around the apartment

A few minutes on foot, you'll find local markets, cafés and direct metro access to reach the
city centre in under 20 minutes.
